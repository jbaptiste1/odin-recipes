Mashed Potatoes

Description

Mashed potato, mashed potatoes (American and Canadian English) or mashed taters, colloquially known as mash (British English),[2] is a dish made by mashing boiled potatoes, usually with added milk, butter, salt and pepper. It is generally served as a side dish to meat or vegetables. When the potatoes are only roughly mashed, they are sometimes called smashed potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Milk
  • Cream Cheese
  • Kosher Salt
  • Black Pepper

Steps

  1. Cut the potatoes
  2. Boil the potatoes
  3. Prepare your melted butter mixture
  4. pan-dry the potatoes
  5. Mash the potatoes
  6. Stir everything together
  7. Taste and season
  8. Serve warm
